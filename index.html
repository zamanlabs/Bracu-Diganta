About Us

BRACU Diganta is a pioneering student organization at BRAC University dedicated to innovation, research, and technological advancement. Founded with the vision to explore new horizons in science and engineering, we are a community of passionate students working on cutting-edge projects in robotics, artificial intelligence, and sustainable technology solutions.

Our mission is to foster a culture of innovation and excellence through collaborative research and development, providing students with opportunities to apply theoretical knowledge in practical, real-world scenarios. We strive to represent BRAC University on both national and international platforms through our projects and competitions.

At BRACU Diganta, we believe in pushing boundaries and challenging conventional thinking. Our team comprises talented individuals from various academic backgrounds who work together to design, develop, and implement innovative solutions to complex problems. Through mentorship, workshops, and hands-on experience, we help members develop both technical and soft skills essential for success in today's competitive world.

Our Achievements

International Competitions

Secured 3rd place in the International Robotics Challenge 2024 held in Singapore, competing against 52 teams from around the world with our autonomous rescue robot "Navigator".

Research & Innovation

Developed "EcoSense" - an AI-powered environmental monitoring system that received the Best Innovation Award at the National Science & Technology Fair 2023 and has been implemented in partnership with the Ministry of Environment.

Community Impact

Launched the "Tech For All" initiative that has conducted over 50 workshops across Bangladesh, training more than 2,000 underprivileged students in basic coding, robotics, and digital literacy skills since 2022.

Academic Excellence

Our members have published 8 research papers in prestigious international journals and conferences in the fields of artificial intelligence, IoT, and sustainable technology, bringing recognition to BRAC University.
